1. Overview

Your privacy is important. The App is designed to minimize data collection and prioritize on-device processing whenever possible.

The App does not require account creation and does not sell or share personal information.

2. Information Stored in the App

The App allows users to create and manage AI prompts and related content.

The following data may be stored locally on your device:

  • Prompt titles and text
  • Tags and organization data
  • Ratings and preferences
  • App settings

This information is stored using Apple system technologies and remains under your control.

3. iCloud / CloudKit Sync

If iCloud synchronization is enabled, your data may be securely stored using Apple CloudKit.

  • Data is associated with your Apple ID.
  • The developer does not have direct access to this data.
  • Apple manages encryption and storage.

Learn more at: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. AI Features

The App may offer AI-based prompt improvement features.

  • Supported devices may process improvements directly on-device using Apple technologies.
  • On unsupported devices, limited processing methods may be used to provide similar functionality.
  • No personal identity information is intentionally collected for AI processing.

5. Keyboard Extension

The App includes an optional keyboard extension.

The keyboard:

  • Displays saved prompts selected by the user
  • Inserts prompts into text fields when tapped

The keyboard does not:

  • record keystrokes
  • collect personal typing data
  • transmit what you type

6. Purchases and Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store.

The developer does not receive payment information such as credit card numbers or billing details.

7. Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use third-party advertising trackers and does not track users across apps or websites.

8. Data Control

You may delete your data at any time by:

  • removing content inside the App, or
  • deleting the App from your device.

9. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the App indicates acceptance of updates.
